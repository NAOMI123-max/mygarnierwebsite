Product Description

Garnier Micellar Cleansing Water gently removes makeup and cleanses skin in one step without rubbing. The micelles act like a magnet to lift away makeup, dirt and impurities without harsh rubbing. No rinsing required. Suitable for all skin types, including sensitive skin.

Dermatologically tested. Hypoallergenic. Paraben-free. Alcohol-free. Fragrance-free.

How to Use:
  1. Apply to a cotton pad until soaked
  2. Gently wipe over face, eyes and lips
  3. No need to rinse
  4. Use morning and evening
